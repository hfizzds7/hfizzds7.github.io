KUROSAKI ICHIGO

A high school student who can see ghosts. Lost his mother as a child to an attack by Hollows. Acquires Soul Reaper powers through an encounter with Rukia Kuchiki and becomes a Substitute Soul Reaper. Through the turmoil that embroiled the World of the Living, the Soul Society, and Hueco Mundo, he gains the trust of the other Soul Reapers and lives up to his position.

URAHARA KISUKE

Once Captain of the Squad 12 and the first chief of the Department of Research and Development. Currently runs a penny candy store called “Urahara’s Shop,” and provides various supplies to Soul Reapers. Supports Ichigo in many ways as he grows as a Soul Reaper.

INOUE ORIHIME

Ichigo's classmate. Possesses a cheerful personality and carefree smile. Often makes zany off-topic remarks, but is always thinking of Ichigo and her friends. Awakens to her spiritual powers after Ichigo rescues her after the Hollowfication of her older brother.

SADO YASUTORA

Ichigo’s good friend since middle school. He is one-quarter Mexican. His friends call him “Chad.” As a child, he often resorted to violence until his grandfather set him straight. Since then, he has refrained from using his strength for selfish reasons. Accompanies Ichigo with the promise he made to him.

SHIHOUIN YORUICHI

Former second division commander. When he was a captain, he was the Commander-in-Chief of Covert Mobile Operations and A powerful man who also served as the general commander of the 1st squad, ``Punishing Army''. He also served as the 22nd head of the ``Shifuin Family,'' one of the four great aristocrats. He is free-spirited and likes to make fun of people. He is an expert in walking and is so strong that he is nicknamed ``Shunshin Yoruichi''. Along with Urahara, he supports Ichigo in various ways.

YAMAMOTO GENRYUSAI SHIGEKUNI

Head Captain of the Thirteen Court Guard Squads and captain of Squad 1. Founder of the Thirteen Court Guard Squads and the highest-ranking of all Soul Reapers. Despite his advanced age, he has trained his body to its limits and boasts overwhelming combat power. Shunsui Kyoraku and Jushiro Ukitake are among his former protégés.

KYORAKU SHUNSUI

Captain of Squad 8. Does not like fighting and enjoys drinking and wearing flashy women’s kimono. While conspicuous by his aloof and frivolous behavior, he is actually prudent and well-liked by those around him. When Rukia is about to be executed, he collaborated with Jushiro Ukitake to destroy the Sokyoku and crossed swords with his master, Shigekuni Genryusai Yamamoto.

UNOHANA RETSU

Captain of Squad 4 whose main mission is rescue. Although possessing a gentle and mild-mannered personality, she is frightening when angered and commands the Soul Reapers’ respect. She was the first to recognize Sosuke Aizen’s conspiracy during the turmoil involving the Soul Society and provide a solution. She often guides Soul Reapers with her calm decision-making.

KUCHIKI BYAKUYA

Captain of Squad 6. Patriarch of the Kuchiki Clan, one of the Four Great Noble Clans and elder brother-in-law of Rukia Kuchiki. As a nobleman, he has disciplined himself to be a role model for all Soul Reapers and rarely shows his emotions, but there is a passion in his heart. Following the turmoil in the Soul Society, he comes to respect the pride and bond between himself and his fellow Soul Reapers.

HITSUGAYA TOSHIRO

A child prodigy who rose to captain of Squad 10 as the youngest captain ever. A skilled fighter and administrator. Despite his youthful appearance, he possesses keen intuition. He acts calmly, but shows his fury at times. He and his lieutenant, Rangiku Matsumoto, are complete opposites, but share a good relationship. Momo Hinamori’s friend since childhood.

KUCHIKI RUKIA

The person who met Ichigo and shared her Soul Reaper powers with him. Rescued by Ichigo when Sosuke Aizen plotted to kill her. Later returned to the squad to battle together with Ichigo and her fellow squad members not just as a Soul Reaper, but as a comrade. Played a role in restoring Ichigo's powers after he lost his Soul Reaper powers. Currently lieutenant of Squad 13.

ABARAI RENJI

Lieutenant of Squad 6. Although a lieutenant, he has mastered Bankai and is as strong as any captain. A friend of Rukia Kuchiki since their Rukon District days; the two are like family. When Rukia's execution was decided, he built a cooperative relationship with Ichigo and worked together to rescue Rukia.

AIZEN SOUSUKE

Once captain of Squad 5 of the Thirteen Court Guard Squads. Responsible for the turmoil involving the Soul Society and Hueco Mundo. Sealed following a battle with the Soul Reapers and Ichigo. Currently imprisoned in the Central Underground Great Prison “Muken.

ISE NANAO

Lieutenant of Squad 8. A very serious and pragmatic person and the complete opposite of her captain, Shunsui Kyoraku. Often treats Shunsui coldly, but in her heart trusts him deeply and devotes herself to being his adjutant.

SOI FON

Captain of Squad 2 as well as captain of Stealth Force, a unit whose main mission is behind-the-scenes operations. She is from the lower class noble clan, the Fon Clan. Reveres Yoruichi Shihoin, her predecessor and former superior. Her intense feelings for her have also been a source of conflict between them, however.

ZARAKI KENPACHI

Captain of Squad 11. A native of “Zaraki” in the Rukon District. Goes by the name “Kenpachi,” a name given to the strongest Soul Reaper of his generation. He lives for sword fighting and considers enjoying battle to be the ultimate pleasure. Wears an eye patch that absorbs and suppresses his spiritual power so that he can enjoy battling adversaries who are less skilled than himself.

YHWACH

Founder of the Quincies and leader of the “Wandenreich.” Addressed as“His Majesty.” Cold and merciless to his followers and does not hesitate to take their lives on the spot.

ISHIDA URYU

Ichigo's classmate. One of the last remaining descendants of the Quincies. Looks up to his late grandfather, Soken as his master. Does not get along with his father, Ryuken, who is adverse to the Quincies. Once despised Ichigo and fought him as an enemy, but later became comrades-in-arms after fighting a battle together.

JUGRAM HASCHWALTH

Commander of the Stern Ritter. Serves Yhwach as his aide. Loyal to Yhwach and is considered to be his successor.

BAMBIETTA BASTERBINE

Stern Ritter. A lively girl.

BAZZARD BLACK

Stern Ritter. Wears his hair in a mohawk. Belligerent and ambitious.

GREMMY THOUMEAUX

Stern Ritter. He is a blond boy who always wears a hood. Even his own body was a "product of his imagination."

LILLE BARRO

Stern Ritter. A member of the "SS". He is confident and has a distinctive marking on his left eye.

ASKIN NAKK LE VAAR

Stern Ritter. He became a member of the "Issue Guards" during the march to the Reiou Palace. He has an easy-going demeanor, but his observational eye is keen.

LILTOTTO LAMPERD

Stern Ritter. A member of "Bumbees". Her nickname is "Lil". She is a petite girl, but she has a sharp tongue that doesn't match her appearance.

STORY

After a brutal surprise attack by the forces of Quincy King Yhwach, the resident Reapers of the Soul Society lick their wounds and mourn their losses. Many of the surviving Soul Reaper captains train to battle without their Bankai, the ultimate technique wielded by the fiercest warriors.
In the previous assault, Ichigo Kurosaki narrowly managed to help fend off Yhwach's fearsome wrath. However, to ultimately defeat his godly adversary and save his allies, Ichigo must now undergo severe training that will push him beyond his physical, emotional, and mental limits.
Though Yhwach already holds the upper hand in this ongoing blood feud, he also successfully recruits Uryuu Ishida, Ichigo's close friend and rival, to be his successor. Yhwach strikes out once again at the weakened Soul Society, intent on finally obliterating his long-standing enemies. As Ichigo struggles to attain new power, the Soul Reaper captains fight for survival and borrowed time.

INTRODUCTION

Originally released as part of the Weekly Shonen Jump lineup, BLEACH the manga continues to capture readers’ hearts throughout the world with over 130 million volumes sold to date. The 366-episode-long epic anime TV series BLEACH found its start back in October of 2004 and led to four feature films. Now, “Thousand-Year Blood War”, the concluding arc of the iconic anime, is finally about to unfold. For the occasion, eminent anime maestro Tomohisa Taguchi brings his unique visual sense as Series Director and Series Composition. Joining the team are BLEACH anime veterans Shiro SAGISU (musician) and Masashi Kudo (character designer). And to bring it all to life once again is studio Pierrot for animation production. The team of talented veterans is ready for the final showdown. What awaits Ichigo Kurosaki?
